AI Citizen Assistance Platform
Legal & Ethical Compliance
1. Legal Disclaimer

The platform provides general legal information to help users understand their rights and available procedures.

All legal information provided by the AI system is for educational and informational purposes only and does not replace professional legal advice from a licensed lawyer.

Users should consult qualified legal professionals for official legal representation or advice.

2. Data Privacy Protection

The platform will protect user data according to privacy principles similar to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (India).

Key privacy rules:

• User personal data will only be collected when necessary.
• Sensitive data will not be stored without explicit user consent.
• All personal data will be encrypted.
• Access to sensitive data will be restricted to authorized personnel only.
• Users may request deletion of their personal data.

Sensitive information may include:

name

phone number

address

identity documents

incident details

3. User Consent Policy

Before collecting personal or incident-related data, the platform will request user consent.

Example consent message:

By continuing, you agree that the platform may process your information to provide assistance and recommendations.

Users will be informed about:

what data is collected

why it is collected

how it will be used

4. Emergency Response Policy

If the system detects a life-threatening or emergency situation, it will immediately guide the user to contact emergency services.

Example emergency situations:

physical violence

threats to life

medical emergencies

kidnapping or abuse

The system will provide verified helpline numbers such as:

Police: 100
National Emergency: 112
Women Helpline: 1091
Child Helpline: 1098
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

The AI system will not attempt to replace emergency responders but will guide users to contact the appropriate authorities.

5. Responsible AI Usage

The AI system must follow responsible AI practices.

Rules include:

• The system should avoid generating incorrect legal advice.
• The system should provide sources when possible.
• The AI must not fabricate laws or legal procedures.
• Sensitive cases may be escalated to human experts.

6. Human Oversight

Certain cases will require human review.

Examples:

serious legal disputes

domestic violence reports

complex legal interpretation

escalated complaints

These cases may be reviewed by:

legal advisors

NGO partners

platform administrators

7. Data Retention Policy

User data will not be stored indefinitely.

Example retention policy:

• general queries – temporary storage
• complaint cases – stored until case resolution
• sensitive incident data – deleted after defined retention period

8. Ethical Guidelines

The platform will follow ethical guidelines to ensure safe use.

The system will:

• prioritize user safety
• avoid harmful or biased responses
• protect vulnerable users
• respect user privacy and confidentiality
