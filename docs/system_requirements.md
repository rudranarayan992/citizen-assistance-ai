Problem Statement

In India, many citizens are not aware of the government welfare schemes that are available for their benefit. Because of this lack of awareness, people often miss opportunities to receive financial help, healthcare benefits, education support, and other services.

Many citizens also do not clearly understand their legal rights. When they face problems such as fraud, harassment, domestic violence, or unfair treatment, they may not know what steps they should take or which authority they should contact.

Another major issue is that crime victims sometimes do not know how to properly report incidents. In some cases, complaints are delayed or ignored, and people do not know how to escalate the issue to higher authorities.

Due to these problems, citizens may feel helpless, and some crimes remain unreported or unresolved.

Aim of the Project

The aim of the AI Citizen Assistance Platform is to create an intelligent system that helps citizens easily access information about government schemes, understand their legal rights, and receive guidance on how to report crimes or seek emergency help.

The platform will act as a digital assistant for citizens, providing quick and simple guidance whenever they need support.

What the Project Solves

This platform will help solve several common problems:
Lack of awareness about government schemes
Lack of knowledge about legal rights
Difficulty in reporting crimes and complaints
Lack of guidance during emergency situations
Delay in getting correct information from authorities

By providing AI-based guidance, the platform will help citizens take the correct actions quickly.

Expected Results

The project aims to create positive social impact by:
Increasing awareness of government services
Helping people understand their legal rights
Making crime reporting easier and faster
Encouraging more victims to report incidents
Improving communication between citizens and authorities

With better guidance and easier reporting systems, the platform can help reduce unreported crimes and support better law enforcement, leading to a safer society.

Features of the Platform

AI Chat Assistant – Answers questions about schemes, legal rights, and emergencies.
Government Scheme Finder – Helps users discover schemes based on age, income, and location.
Legal Rights Guide – Explains common legal rights in simple language.
Crime Reporting Guidance – Shows steps to file complaints and escalate issues.
Emergency Help Section – Provides important helpline numbers and safety instructions.
Multilingual Support – Supports multiple languages for better accessibility.
User-Friendly Interface – Simple design that works on mobile and web devices.

3. System Architecture
Frontend

User interface where citizens interact with the platform through chat, scheme search, and help sections.
Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React

Backend

Handles user requests, processes data, and connects the AI system with the database.
Technologies: Node.js / Python / Django / Flask

AI Assistant Module

Processes user questions using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and provides relevant responses.

Database

Stores information such as:
Government schemes
Legal information
Emergency contacts
User queries

Technologies: MySQL / MongoDB / PostgreSQL

Basic System Flow

User → Frontend → Backend → AI Module + Database → Response to User
